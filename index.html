Frequently Asked Questions

What is a campus representative?

Your campus rep is your first point of contact for anything union-related: workplace concerns, contract issues, or organizing efforts.

How do I contact my rep?

Just select your campus from the dropdown menu above. Your reps' phone numbers and emails will appear instantly.

What if I don’t see my department listed?

Reach out to any rep listed — they can direct you to the correct contact or escalate your question internally.

How often does the union meet?

Each campus hosts regular meetings. Stay tuned to updates through your rep or official Teamsters communications.
